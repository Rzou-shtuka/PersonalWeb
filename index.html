About

+

+ Welcome to my academic homepage. I am an Associate Professor of Mathematics at Purdue University. My + research focuses on algebraic geometry and its interactions with combinatorics and representation theory. + I am particularly interested in the geometry of moduli spaces and applications to enumerative problems. +

+

+ Outside of research and teaching, I am involved in organizing several departmental outreach activities and + mentoring undergraduate research groups through the PRiME program. +

Research

+

+ My research is motivated by understanding moduli of higher-dimensional varieties and their birational + geometry. Recent projects include studying wall-crossing phenomena for Bridgeland stability conditions and + applications of tropical methods to questions in enumerative geometry. +

+

+ If you are interested in collaborating, please reach out via email. I am always eager to talk with students + and colleagues about related problems and new ideas. +
